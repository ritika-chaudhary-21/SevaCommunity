    Household Waste

      For general household waste that is not recyclable or compostable.

    Recycling Waste

      For materials that can be recycled such as paper, cardboard, plastics, glass, and metal.

    Green Waste

      For organic waste such as grass clippings, leaves, branches, and food scraps that can be composted.

    Hazardous Waste

      For materials that are hazardous to health or the environment, such as batteries, chemicals, paints, and oils.

    Garbage Collection Schedule

    Regular Collection Days:

    Monday: Household Waste Collection

    Wednesday: Recycling Collection

    Friday: Green Waste Collection

    Sunday: Hazardous Waste Collection

    Additional Information:

    Please ensure that garbage is properly separated and placed in the designated bins for collection. Make sure to check local guidelines for any special disposal instructions for hazardous waste or bulky items.
